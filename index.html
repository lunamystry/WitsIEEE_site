Welcome to the New IEEE Wits Student Branch Website

 

Welcome to the new website of our Student Branch. This website was officially launched in July 2010 and as such is still evolving to meet the communication needs of our branch.

On this site you will find information about the IEEE and, in particular, the Wits IEEE Student Branch in the About section.

Information regarding the executive committee of the Student Branch for 2010 and 2011 is available on the Committee page.

The latest news and announcements for the branch are made on the News page and details of upcoming events and activities are published on the Events page.

The Gallery shows some pictures from the previous activities of the Student Branch.

Finally, this site also provides information about how to Join or Contact the Wits IEEE Student Branch.

Visit of IEEE President-Elect

 

The IEEE Wits Student Branch, the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) and IEEE Control Systems Society (CSS) of the IEEE South Africa Section, are pleased to host IEEE President-Elect, Professor Moshe Kam, who will visit the University of the Witwatersrand on 9 September 2010.

The President-Elect will be giving a technical talk in the Chamber of mines building (CM2). The topic of the talks is: Decision Integration and Robot Navigation Research at Drexel Universityâ€™s Data Fusion Laboratory. It will start at 13h20 and end at 14H15 on Thursday the 9th of September. Entrance is free and refreshments will be served.

If you are interested in attending this event you MUST register at the following URL: http://meetings.vtools.ieee.org/meeting_registration/register/3075

Something interesting:
Professor Moshe Kam, is one of three presidents of the IEEE at the moment. The IEEE has three presidents at a given point in time. There is the president-elect. The current president and the past president. The president-elect is elected in the general election that is held annually. The current president's term of office ends on the 1st of October.
